Blueprint: Conservative Maintenance

A Pod Disruption Budget (PDB) is a safety contract with Kubernetes. It ensures that voluntary maintenance actions, like node drains, do not take down too many application replicas at once, thus preserving service availability.

Voluntary vs. Involuntary Disruptions

It's critical to understand that PDBs only protect against voluntary disruptions (e.g., node upgrades). They cannot prevent involuntary disruptions caused by unavoidable events like hardware failure.

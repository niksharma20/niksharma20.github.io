Blueprint: Application Health

For Kubernetes to perform its self-healing magic, it needs to understand your application's health. Probes are the mechanism for this, forming a three-part "contract" that defines the application's lifecycle state and tells Kubernetes how to respond.

The Three-Probe Contract

Failing to provide health probes is a critical anti-pattern. Each of the three probes tells Kubernetes a different piece of information about the application's state, dictating the correct automated action to take.
